Classic Cocktails

MANHATTAN

2 ounces rye whiskey, 1 ounce sweet vermouth, 2 dashes Angostura bitters, brandied cherry or lemon twist as garnish

NEGRONI

1 ounce gin, 1 ounce campari, 1 ounce sweet vermouth

MARTINI

2 1/2 hounces gin, 1/2 ounce dry vermouth, 1 dash orange bitters, lemon twist garnish

DAIQUIRI

2 ounces light rum, 1 ounce lime juice, 3/4 ounce demerara sugar syrup, lime twist garnish
